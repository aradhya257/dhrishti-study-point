About Us

A room built around one rule: quiet.

Dhrishti Study Point is a self-study space in Salarpur, near Mata Mai Mandir, Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. We are not a library that lends books — we are a dedicated, disciplined study room where students bring their own material and simply focus, for as many hours as they need.

The idea came from a simple, common problem: home is full of distractions, and public libraries in Varanasi are often noisy or crowded during exam season. Dhrishti Study Point solves this with individually assigned seating, a strict silent environment, and long, exam-friendly hours — every single day.

Our Mission

To give every serious student in Varanasi access to a calm, well-equipped place to study — regardless of how noisy or crowded their home environment is.

Our Approach

Assigned seats, tracked attendance, and a no-talking policy — small rules that add up to hours of genuinely uninterrupted study time.

What Studying Here Feels Like

You walk in, sit at your assigned desk, and the room does the rest. No phone calls, no group chatter, no distractions — just the quiet hum of focused study around you. Many of our students say the environment itself keeps them accountable, even on days when motivation is low.
